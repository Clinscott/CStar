# 🔱 QMD SEARCH SKILL (v1.0)

## When to Use
- Use when searching PennyOne QMD reports across Gungnir vectors to identify weak sectors and feed bead generation.


## MANDATE
The Scryer of the Hall of Records. This skill parses the `.stats/*.qmd` files generated by PennyOne to analyze the 10 Gungnir vectors (Logic, Style, Intel, Gravity, Vigil, Stability, Coupling, Aesthetic, Anomaly, Sovereignty) and identify the weakest sectors of the Corvus Star matrix.

## CORE PRINCIPLES
1. **The Source of Truth**: `.stats/*.qmd` files are the only authoritative source of system health.
2. **Decalogue Scrying**: Search by specific Gungnir metrics to find targeted weaknesses (e.g., "Find high coupling", "Find low intel").
3. **Bead Generation Feeder**: Output is structured to directly feed Muninn and Huginn for `norn_beads` creation.

## INTERFACE
`cstar qmd-search --metric <metric_name> --limit <N>`
