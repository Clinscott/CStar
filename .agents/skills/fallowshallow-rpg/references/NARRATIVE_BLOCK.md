# Fallows Hallow: Narrative Block & Storyboard
**Status:** Phase 1 (Draft)
**Lead Architect Persona:** SAKAGUCHI

## 1. The Overarching Story (The Golden Thread)
The narrative is a tragedy of exploited innocence. A group of children (the Chroni lineage) are violently pulled from their mundane lives into the "Old World," a dark, magical realm. Here, they discover they are batteries—their lineage and latent powers are being harvested to maintain the cosmic prison of Mathias, the forgotten brother. The story is their struggle to survive, escape slavery, and decide whether to break the cycle (freeing Mathias) or maintain the prison at the cost of their own lives.

## 2. The Three Acts

### Act I: The Night of Conquest (Innocence Lost)
*   **Mood:** Autumnal, nostalgic, eerie, sudden violence.
*   **Story Beats:**
    1.  **The Mundane:** Halloween night. John, Spence, Alfred, Gideon, Nicci, and the Twins prepare for the Fallows' haunted house.
    2.  **The Breach:** The haunted house is a trap. The "Old World" bleeds into reality. The fire breaks out.
    3.  **The Tragedy:** Spence is killed in the panic (the catalyst).
    4.  **The Abduction:** Nicci is torn away by Nikita. The rest are captured and sold into slavery.

### Act II: The Diaspora (The Forging of the Tapestry)
*   **Mood:** Oppressive, disparate, survival-focused, brutal.
*   **Story Beats:**
    1.  **Separation:** The group is split. Gideon to the Arena, Alfred to the Chroni scholars, the Twins to the Harem. John is left to wander/search.
    2.  **Awakening:** The children awaken their latent powers out of pure survival. (John discovers Temporal Sight, Gideon unlocks the Martial Singularity).
    3.  **Reunion (The Arena):** John discovers Gideon fighting as "The Beaut." A desperate escape plan is forged.
    4.  **Gathering the Threads:** The party slowly reforms, rescuing Alfred and the Twins, and discovering the truth of the "Lagi Tapestry"—that their memories are fuel.

### Act III: The Martyr's Choice (The Full Circle)
*   **Mood:** Grand, tragic, cosmic, resolute.
*   **Story Beats:**
    1.  **The Truth of the Hallow:** The party learns Selina and Roan/Brian orchestrated their suffering to keep Mathias imprisoned.
    2.  **The Temptation:** Mathias reaches out, offering immense power to break his cage.
    3.  **The Confrontation:** The final assault on the Hallow.
    4.  **The Choice:** The party must choose how to utilize their forged memories. Do they sacrifice themselves to maintain the prison (The Canonical Ending), or do they unleash Mathias to destroy the Old World?

## 3. Combining Stories & Character Arcs
*   **John:** The burden of survival. His temporal powers mean he must watch his friends suffer, knowing he is the only one "intended" to survive.
*   **Gideon:** The loss of identity. He becomes a weapon ("The Beaut"). His arc is reclaiming his humanity from the trauma of the pits.
*   **Nicci:** The corrupted matriarch. Taken early, her story is one of adapting to the harsh nature of the Old World, returning as a hardened, wild force.
*   **Alfred:** The curse of foresight. He sees the tragic end but must guide the party toward it anyway.
*   **The Twins:** The loss of individuality. Their survival mechanic (body takeover) threatens to merge their minds permanently.
