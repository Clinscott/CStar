# 🔱 RESEARCH SKILL (v1.0)

## MANDATE
Conduct autonomous, fixed-budget experimentation to optimize target logic. This skill implements the "Karpathy Standard" for agent-driven research.

## CORE PRINCIPLES
1. **The Crucible**: Every experiment is bound by a fixed time budget (default: 300s).
2. **Fixed Environment**: The evaluation logic and data constants are IMMUTABLE.
3. **Hypothesis First**: The One Mind must document a hypothesis before every strike.
4. **Neuralplastic Evolution**: Successes are crystallized into the target's Gherkin behavioral contract.

## INTERFACE
`cstar research <target_file> --budget <seconds> --metric <name>`
