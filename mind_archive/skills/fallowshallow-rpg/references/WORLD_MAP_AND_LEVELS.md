# Fallows Hallow: World Map & Level Design Spec
**Status:** Phase 1 (Draft)
**Lead Architect Persona:** MIYAZAKI / WRIGHT

## 1. The World Map Structure
The game uses a classic "Hub and Spoke" world map, but with a Miyazaki-inspired interconnectedness. The map represents the "Old World," a decaying, ancient landscape layered over the ruins of forgotten civilizations.

### The Macro Flow:
1.  **The Prologue Hub:** The Mundane Town (Linear, Safe).
2.  **The Breach:** The Fallows' Hallow (Dungeon, Transition).
3.  **The Old World (Act II & III):** Open overworld map, unlocking regions as the party reunites.

## 2. Key Regions & Level Design

### 2.1 The Fallows' Hallow (The Breach)
*   **Mood Board:** Burning autumn leaves, pitch-black corridors, distorted Victorian architecture.
*   **Level Design:** A twisting, non-euclidean haunted house. Corridors loop back on themselves. 
*   **Scene Flow:** Starts as a spooky Halloween attraction. Slowly, the assets corrupt. Fake blood becomes real. The final room is the "Fire Room," a boss encounter that the player is *meant to lose*, leading to the Diaspora.

### 2.2 The Slave Pits of the Arena (Gideon's Crucible)
*   **Mood Board:** Dust, blood, harsh sunlight, rusted iron, despair.
*   **Level Design:** A coliseum surrounded by tight, labyrinthine holding cells. 
*   **Scene Flow:** John infiltrates the cells (stealth/puzzle elements). The reunion scene with Gideon happens in the center of the Arena during a gladiatorial combat encounter.

### 2.3 The Chroni Citadel (Alfred's Prison)
*   **Mood Board:** Towering spires of obsidian, floating tomes, cold blue arcane light, sterile silence.
*   **Level Design:** Vertical exploration. Puzzles involving Alfred's "Foresight" mechanic to manipulate moving platforms and bypass magical wards.
*   **Scene Flow:** A heist. The party must extract Alfred while navigating the scholars' temporal traps.

### 2.4 The Pleasure House / Distant Harem (The Twins' Gilded Cage)
*   **Mood Board:** Opulence hiding rot, heavy silks, incense smoke, claustrophobic luxury.
*   **Level Design:** A social stealth/investigation area. Combat is rare but deadly if caught.
*   **Scene Flow:** The Twins must use their "Takeover" mechanic to bypass guards and coordinate a simultaneous dual-character escape sequence.

### 2.5 The Deep Woods (Nicci's Domain)
*   **Mood Board:** Overgrown, ancient roots, feral beasts, glowing flora.
*   **Level Design:** A dense, confusing forest maze.
*   **Scene Flow:** The party is hunted. Nicci reveals herself not as a captive, but as the apex predator of the woods, saving the party in a dramatic set-piece.

### 2.6 The Core of the Hallow (Mathias's Prison)
*   **Mood Board:** Absolute darkness pierced by blinding white light, shattered chains, temporal distortion.
*   **Level Design:** The final dungeon. A surreal amalgamation of all previous areas and memories.
*   **Scene Flow:** The ultimate test of the "Lagi Tapestry." Players must sacrifice their forged memories to progress through the final barriers toward Selina and Roan/Brian.
